## LTCAL032
### Overview
- This COBOL program, `LTCAL032`, is a subroutine designed to calculate Long-Term Care (LTC) payments based on the DRG (Diagnosis Related Group) for bills. It receives bill data, provider information, and wage index data, and returns the calculated payment information. It incorporates logic for normal DRG payments, short stay payments, and outliers. It also includes blending logic for different facility payment scenarios.
